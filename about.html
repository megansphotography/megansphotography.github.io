Megan is a student at North Allegheny Senior High School. She is 18 and currently working as a hostess in Wexford. She plans on attending college next year but is unsure of where as of right now. Megan found her passion for photography last year when she took Photography 1 taught by Mr. Clemmer at NA. She uses Adobe Photoshop to edit pictures and a Canon EOS Rebel T1i to take them. Other activites Megan is involved in are the NA Marching Band, Indoor Percussion, Key Club and plans on joining the Ultimate Frisbee team this spring.


She has won two awards for her photography. A gold and silver key in the Scholastic Art and Writing Competition last year. She has plans to enter again this year. Although she doesn't see a career in photography it will always be a favorite hobby. Megan was also named an 'up and coming' photographer by her school's online newspaper the NASH Uproar.
